Features

Everything You Need to Scrape Shopify

Designed for dropshippers, marketers, and developers who need clean data fast.

📦

Product Extraction

Navigate to any Shopify product page and pull the full payload — title, price, variants, tags, images, and descriptions — in one click.

📊

CSV & JSON Export

Export data as Excel-ready CSV spreadsheets or raw JSON. Our custom engine handles nested objects, HTML stripping, and UTF-8 encoding natively.

🖼️

Image ZIP Bundles

Download every product image in full resolution, compressed into a single ZIP file. No more right-click-saving one image at a time.

🎨

Brand Kit Extractor

Scrape a competitor's logo, favicon, brand colors, theme name, and social media links — packaged into a clean ZIP with a full report.

📑

Collection Crawler

Browsing a specific collection page? Extract only the products visible on your screen. Our batched engine fetches them 10 at a time for maximum speed.

⚙️

Customizable Pipeline

Strip HTML from descriptions, exclude columns, choose filename conventions — all configurable from a dedicated Settings dashboard synced across browsers.

How It Works

Three Steps. Zero Servers.

Your data never leaves your browser. No accounts, no API keys, no subscriptions.

Browse a Shopify Store

Navigate to any Shopify-powered website. The extension automatically detects the store and lights up.

Choose Your Extraction

Pick a single product, visible collection items, or the entire catalog. Select CSV or JSON. Clean or Raw.

Download Instantly

Your file downloads directly to your computer. Images get zipped. Brand assets get packaged. Done.
